About

Welcome to Kiwi Clotheslines, the Auckland wide clothesline installation and repair experts, We stock quality Hills Clotheslines with a 10 year warranty. In addition we do not charge for travel in most instances and with competitive pricing we often can charge less including instalation for a clothesline not installed. We travel from Owera to Pukekoe and service the wider and inner Auckland region.

If you need a new clothesline and you want to save money call me. Save money on installations of new and factory seconds/damaged carton quality clotheslines from Hills. As a builder with 30 years experience 6 years of these installing Clotheslines, Hills is the only brand I trust. We also do removals repairs & rewires to all ranges of existing clotheslines. We stock the full range of Hills clotheslines Galvanised rotary, Powder coated rotary, Extendaline & Paraline models. In addition most clotheslines can be installed, repaired and rewired within a few days.
